    How to Play Grid Golf

    Welcome to Grid Golf! The goal is to get golf ball into each of the holes in as few swings as possible.

    Game Objective

    Get the ball into each hole using the fewest strokes possible.

    Course

    1. Golf course have 3 holes. Hole need to be complete one after another.
    2. Grid: The game is played on a grid. Your ball and the holes are positioned on this grid.
    3. Obstacles: Watch out for obstacles like rocks, bushes, and sand traps! Hitting an obstacle will stop your ball.

    Controls

    1. Direction Buttons: Use the direction buttons (↖, ⬆, ↗, ⬅, ⮕, ↙, ⬇, ↘) to choose the direction you want to hit the ball.
    2. Power: After choosing a direction, the game will randomly determine the power of your shot. The power level is indicated by the red bar on the side.
    3. Ball Movement: The ball moves on the grid based on the chosen direction and power.

    Scoring

    1. Par: Each hole has a "par" number, which is the expected number of swings to complete the hole.
    2. Score Terms:
      • Better than Par: "Birdie", "Eagle", "Albatross" (lower score is better!)
      • Equal to Par: "Par"
      • Worse than Par: "Bogey", "Double Bogey", "Triple Bogey", "Bad Putter"
    3. Total Score: Your total score is the sum of your swings for all holes. Try to get the lowest total score! (high score is worst)

    How to Win:

    Complete all holes with the lowest possible total score! Good luck and have fun playing Grid Golf!
